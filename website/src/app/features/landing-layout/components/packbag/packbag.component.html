Our Timeless Classics

“There’s something so beautiful about a sari, that each time I try to pen it down, I’m lost for words. It’s a timeless classic. My wardrobe is adorned with my grand-mother’s silk Sarees, which I borrowed from my mum and never returned. Complete with a simple string of pearls or polkis or dressed up with a heavy choker, a Saree can be as versatile as we want it to be.”
